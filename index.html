Places I Like

FAGUN RESTAURANT

I had visited in December 2022

Awesome service in this restaurant.Great ambiance good food lots of variety in food and staff was polite and helpful. Nothing is too much for them. They really want you to be happy and will do almost anything to meet your needs. Will definitely like to dine here again.



CHAPPAN DUKAN

I had visited in January 2023

A unique idea of having 56 shops each serving different variety of food. Like we have chappan bhog for the gods this is for us. The kachoris are a must have here you cannot just miss them and the rest of the fare was also decent
